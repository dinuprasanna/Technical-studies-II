This is an attempt to establish a serial connection between Arduino and processing. The Story Honestly, I didn't have any projects or ideas in my mind that involved serial communication. Yet I was very involved in making this. So I questioned why did I want to do this. I realised I always like connecting stuff. Be it academics or sports, I always like to explore new things and try to connect them together. It also expands my two isolated Arduino and Processing skillsets into one huge, connected, powerful prototyping skill. I tried to send two random values from Processing to Arduino and display them on the LCD. There were a lot of sources online about serial communication, but I did not find a lot of references to send arrays. So, I decided to take a break and have chai. Then, finally figured out a way to do so. This made me realise that consistently making stuff without fear is the only way to get comfortable with unfamiliar technology without intimidation.
