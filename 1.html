LED matrix responds according to the data from Ultrasonic sensor. If someone is closer it lights up patterns. The Story The concept of LED matrix is inherently interesting to explore. 64 LEDs seemed very complex, and I hate complexity. So that is why I decided to decode it and make things simple. By the end, I figured out how arrays worked and got to control each LED at my own convenience. I hate complexity, but I don't move away from them. I try to break them into simple things. Thats one of the main reasons to explore this component.
